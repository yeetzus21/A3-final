Community Fundraising

Once you get started, you may be surprised at just how easy it can be! We've provided some thought starters to help you make the most of your fundraiser.

Netballers, sports clubs, workplaces and schools are all uniting to join TEAM CRAZY!

Cupcakes 4 a Cure

Bake a difference for childhood cancer research.

Lose Your Locks

Clip, cut or chop your hair for a good cause!

Dress up like a pirate, bring your treasure, and raise money for kids' brain cancer research.

A fun, creative and collaborative competition for students in years 5 to 12 and adult writing groups.

Fundraise Yourself

Let's raise funds together and make a difference. Whether you want to dress up, bake a difference, or just go crazy you can be a community champion by fundraising with The Kids' Cancer Project. We'll support you every step of the way in whatever you choose to do.
